Gluten Free Banana Pancakes

Description

Easy delicious banana pancakes to start your morning.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup gluten free flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Combine mashed bananas and eggs in a bowl. Whisk together.
  2. Stir in gluten free flour and baking soda. Batter will be lumpy.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium heat. Pour a scoop of batter onto the griddle using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until the pancakes are golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes on each side. Serve hot.
